Golden Retriever


The golden retriever is a very popular opinion as it is very good with kids its playful fun and friendly nature makes it a perfect match for kids.
It is also brilliant so it's good for parents who like training their dogs.


Labrador (Retriever)

Labrador LOVE swimming it was like they were made to swim and it helps calm their endless energy!
Thanks to their eagerness to please and willingness to work hard they are good service workers who also double as a lovable pet!


Poodle

These dogs are amongst some of the smartest dogs in the world, this, combined with their love for familys makes the perfect family dog.
However, sometimes they can be very emotionally sensitive so they aren't good for a tired person.


Pug


Pugs are cute and lovable companions and their favorite thing is to be with their owner!
They are wonderful for people who have a lot of time on their hands to care for them.


Shih Tzu

Shih Tzus are known for their perky and happy temperaments. They are very playful love being around humans and are very loyal dogs.
Despite their small nature, they love exercise and should get around 30mins to 1 hour of exercise daily.
